Become a vendor

Notice

Please read every rule we list here, make sure to agree and if you agree, you may fill out the form below.

We take a vendor fee in height of 650€ per application. You will have to deposit the XMR in advance to your wallet on Archetyp. The vendor fee of 650€ in XMR is currently ~ 3.13493 XMR

Rules & Information

1. Please include proof images, you can upload files to filehole.org, make sure to remove the metadata and handle the images like product images on Archetyp. Add Username+Archetyp+Current Date to the image.

2. Please fill out an answer to every question asked, you may respond with "no".

3. If we deny your application, we will not refund you the application fee, the fee is not refundable. If you do not provide images as requested, do not apply, just leave us, we will not refund you neither grant you vendor rights.

4. If you are accepted as a vendor, make sure to use common sense. (Don't scam users, don't cheat / lie, don't manipulate users, ...)

5. If you need to contact "the admins TM" you don't send PM's but visit /help and create a support ticket

6. You only use mirrors from https://archetyp.cc or from /settings -> Private mirror to access the site, make sure to not get phished and use 2-FA at all times.

7. You never send any sensitive details in cleartext, communication of everything sensitive has to be encrypted at all times. You use a fresh account to apply, not linked to any other accounts you may own.

8. You don't spam the market with listings, this is not a shit market, Archetyp is a quality e-commerce platform and we demand respect. (You don't create 100 listings but instead 10 and make use of the listing options for the price, if in doubt write a ticket to /help)

9. You don't spam the forum either, you may create one (1) single thread in /b/vendors/threads but only AFTER you were accepted to the market as a vendor.

10. You read the FAQs, new Announcements and the patchnotes and try to be a helpful user of this community, you are friendly and read the rules located under /help too.
