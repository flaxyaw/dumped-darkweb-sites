Good to know

Now that you verified your deposit address, we will explain you the basics of Archetyp

On Archetyp we only use Monero

You can download different Monero wallets here: https://getmonero.org - it's the official website of the Monero project. Monero can also be referred to with its signal XMR.

The best practice is to send Monero not directly from an exchange to the market but to first transfer it to your own wallet. Exchange -> own wallet -> Market This makes sure that the exchange does not know, that you are sending money to a darknet market.

PGP - Pretty good privacy

To encrypt sensitive details, we use PGP, it's always recommend to encrypt everything yourself and not to rely on anyone else. We have a fail-safe for shipping details that will automatically encrypt them if you forget to do it yourself, but you should always do it yourself. We have tutorials for PGP inside of the forum and the ArcheWiki.

Its easy to learn PGP and will increase your security greatly. Make a cup of coffee, learn PGP, get comfortable with it.

Payment systems

We have two payment systems on Archetyp: A classic wallet where you first deposit to the market before you order and a direct pay system, where you order and pay afterwards.

Furthermore we have Escrow and FE listings. FE means finalize early, this way the vendor directly is paid and you are not protected by our escrow. We recommend to always use escrow.

Support system

We have a support system and a great support team. You access it via /help -> Support.

We usually respond very fast and in far less than 24h. Please accept that it can take sometimes more time till you get a response. You can ask questions in our forum too, where every user is able to respond to you.

Your first deposit

Your first deposit to the market should be a couple cents, make sure they land here before you deposit more. Deposits are credited after 10 confirmations, to refresh your balance you can directly visit your /wallet

If a deposit was not credited and the transaction got over 20 confirmations, you can also use our TXID-Recovery tool. It can be used by entering the TXID / Transaction-ID of your deposit which will automatically deposit your funds in case they went missing. You can find the tool also in your /wallet

Anti-Phishing Jail

After login you will first get redirected to the Anti-Phishing Jail, the jail will show you, your display username inside of a rectangle. You will have to click onto it to proceed.

Please make sure to compare the URL you are browsing with the one inside of the image. It will show you the start and the end and they both have to match.

Orders & Disputes

Every listing and shipping method can have different auto-finalize times (escrow only), you can see the time inside of the order process, it is written next to the shipping option.

You can extend the timer three times. So before you dispute an order, please use the extend feature.
