Vacation mode

In vacation mode, in case you are away from keyboard for a while, you can disable your profile, listings and related items until re-activation.

This mode will be enabled automatically after 7 days of inactivity.

PGP Two-Factor-Authentication

2FA adds a level of security to your account as access will only be granted with your credentials and a confirmation that you have the corresponding private key and (optional) passphrase.

A valid PGP public key needs to be setup for your account prior to this.

No image mode

With image loading being disabled, no images will be displayed on the page anymore. Images are for example being used in the product listings. This results in less traffic, faster page loads and slightly improved performance.

Account deletion

We're sorry to hear about your decision, but you are always free to permanently remove your account from our platform. Keep in mind any remaining funds will be lost if not successfully sent to an external wallet before deleting.
