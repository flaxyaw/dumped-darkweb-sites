Please keep in mind that you can always credit missed deposits that have more than 20 confirmations yourself, there is no need for a ticket. Just enter the TXID / Transaction ID / TX hash (out) into the form below and complete the captcha. You will get a notification at the top of the page afterwards that will let you know what happened.

The Monero-TXID is alphanumeric and looks like this: 65c570bda89bb112b78b08fe5b71cef3ef50ba6313e92618fe6c186cf527ec92

20 confirmations are needed for this to work

If you are missing a XMR withdrawal from Archetyp to your own wallet, please check on /wallet/history if you can see your withdrawal and if the XMR address you have withdrawn to, is correct. If yes, create a ticket below and add the time of your withdrawal, then we can send you the TXID to confirm it.
If you have an unfunded order, but you deposited Monero / XMR to the market, usually you can just wait 15 minutes and it will be funded automated.
You can also visit /orders/payments and refresh the page every 3 minutes to check if anything happens.
You can also check your /wallet if you see the deposited XMR in your available balance. If you do not see it, we recommend to use the TXID recovery tool (located on /wallet or missing deposit page), to credit the funds automated to your account, afterwards refresh /orders/payments and your order should change the status. If the three hours are already over, and your order got deleted, you can re-order.
Visit your /wallet and you should see the funds, visiting /wallet refresh your balance on the market.
If you have troubles with withdrawing your balance from Archetyp, please read this guide on how to withdraw: /post/SzNjdys5THdtbDh4YldZdytjM2JRQT09.

If you need the PIN reset, you can do this via /reset (after reset, there is a 5 day waiting time till you can withdraw)

If your withdrawal was blocked for 10 days, please read this post and note that it's actually just 5 days: /post/RlpRWlVZN3prWTU2UVdYY2lCWFFxdz09
This system was created to prevent phishers from withdrawing victim's funds from their accounts giving them time to contact staff and recover the account. You will be able to withdraw your funds to your wallet in 5 days.

This is the system:
On every withdrawal, you start with the score: 0
-> Changed PIN / Password recently? +1
-> Changed PGP Public Key recently? +1
-> Cancelled order recently? +1
-> Recent deposit? +1
-> Suspicious account? +1

If your score is 2 or higher, we block your withdrawal.
The dispute has a certain process that must be walked through to avoid rash decisions.
You can read about our dispute process here: /post/cUJwcU52RHhRSGFyUHRkTS9NUmRYUT09
In short, you can request a moderator after seven days (7x24h) by clicking the 'Request Moderator' button in the dispute.
A moderator will then help with the dispute and only if necessary make a decision. But before that, the vendor and buyer are encouraged to reach an agreement between themselves. As a buyer it's up to you to decide if you agree or disagree with a vendors T&C's, when placing a order you agree to their terms. Sadly waiting is a part of disputes, as it's a complicated process.

Unfortunately, you have let the order finalize, which results in the funds being moved from our escrow wallet to the vendors wallet. Once the funds leave escrow, there is nothing we can do. We cannot reverse this process.

You can normally extend it 3 times to up to 15 days extra to keep the money in escrow. If a package has still not arrived after this time, you can dispute the order to freeze the funds. You can read about our dispute process here: /post/cUJwcU52RHhRSGFyUHRkTS9NUmRYUT09

We recommend to keep trying to get in contact with vendors. Keep in mind that vendors also have a life and other customers, so they might not always reply as fast as you wish.

Rules

While this is a "darknet" marketplace, this is not a place without rules and values.

Do's

Be friendly. Treat others the way you want to be treated. Good behavior is always encouraged.

Be fair. Provide fair and honest feedback, understand possible issues on the other end.

Be honest. Describe your goods properly, tell others the truth while maintaining your OPSEC standards.

Be helpful. Report violations, provide guidance for those in need. We are stronger together.

Don'ts

Sell forbidden goods. Everything related to porn, racism, weapons, poisons, fraud and scam is strictly forbidden.

Hurt individuals. Personal data, doxing, data of individual persons is strictly forbidden on the platform.

Be evil. Insults, personal attacks or any similar negative behavior is not being tolerated.
